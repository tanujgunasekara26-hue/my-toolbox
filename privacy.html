🔒 Privacy Policy

How My Toolbox handles your data and privacy

Application: My Toolbox Desktop Application  |  Last Updated: June 20, 2026  |  Effective: Upon Installation
✅ GOOD NEWS: My Toolbox does NOT collect, store, or transmit your personal data. There is no account registration, no telemetry, and no tracking of any kind.

1. What We Do NOT Collect

My Toolbox is designed with privacy first. We do NOT collect:

  • ✅ Personal identifying information (name, email, address)
  • ✅ Usage analytics or telemetry
  • ✅ Keystroke or screen recording data
  • ✅ Location or IP address data
  • ✅ Browser or device fingerprints
  • ✅ Files or documents you process
  • ✅ Notes or content you create
  • ✅ Crash reports or diagnostics
  • ✅ Browsing history or habits

2. What the App Stores Locally

Some data is stored locally on your computer only — never sent to us:

  • 📝 Notes (saved in plaintext on your device)
  • 📝 App preferences and settings
  • 📝 Generated QR codes (not stored unless saved by you)
⚠️ WARNING: Local files are stored in plaintext with NO encryption. Do not store sensitive personal information in the Notes tool.

3. Third-Party Services

Some features connect to third-party services. When you use these features, data is sent to those services — NOT to us.

🤖 Cerebras API (AI Chatbot & Writing Tools)

What is sent: Your text prompts and messages
Purpose: AI language model processing
Geographic restriction: United States only
Their Privacy Policy: cerebras.net/privacy
Note: Cerebras may log or store your prompts per their own policy. Review it carefully.

💱 Frankfurter API (Currency Converter)

What is sent: Currency conversion queries
Purpose: Real-time exchange rate data
Data source: European Central Bank
Their Website: frankfurter.app
Note: Queries are minimal and contain no personal data.

4. Your Rights

  • ✅ You can delete locally stored files at any time
  • ✅ Uninstalling the Software removes the app (local data files may remain)
  • ✅ No account to delete — we hold no data about you
  • ✅ No opt-out needed — we never collected data to begin with

5. Security

Since we don't collect data, there is no server-side data to breach. However, your local files are only as secure as your computer. We recommend:

  • 🔐 Use full-disk encryption (BitLocker)
  • 🔐 Use a strong computer login password
  • 🔐 Keep your OS and antivirus updated
  • 🔐 Do not store sensitive data in the Notes tool

6. Children's Privacy

My Toolbox is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from anyone, including children. The Software should be used by adults or with parental supervision.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy at any time. The updated version will be reflected on this page with a new "Last Updated" date. Continued use of the Software constitutes acceptance of any changes.

8. Contact

For privacy concerns or questions, contact the Developer via the GitHub repository.
