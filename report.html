📋 Known Issues

  • AI features not available outside US
    AI Chatbot and Writing Tools are restricted to the United States due to API limitations.
    Known
  • Currency rates may be delayed
    Exchange rates update daily. Real-time rates are not supported.
    By Design
  • Sample media not showing in Retro Windows
    Media files must be placed in correct subfolders (pictures/, videos/, music/, recorded_tv/).
    Fixed in v2.0
  • Duplicate entries in Sample Pictures
    Pictures were shown twice if files existed in both version folder and pictures/ subfolder.
    Fixed in v2.0
  • App may be flagged by antivirus
    Newly built .exe files are sometimes flagged by antivirus as suspicious. This is a false positive.
    Investigating
