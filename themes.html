🎨 Themes

Customize your My Toolbox experience with beautiful themes

Overview

My Toolbox comes with 5 professionally designed themes to suit your preference and work environment. Each theme includes both light and dark variants, so you can switch based on time of day or mood.

💡 Pro Tip: Switch themes anytime from Settings → Appearance → Theme. The change takes effect immediately on the next restart.

5 Built-In Themes

☀️

Light

Clean, minimal, bright interface perfect for daytime and well-lit environments.

🌙

Dark

Easy on the eyes, modern dark mode. Perfect for late-night work and reducing eye strain.

💼

Blue

Professional corporate blue. Modern, clean, and business-ready.

🌿

Green

Fresh, energetic, and calming. Great for long work sessions.

✨

Purple

Creative and artistic. Perfect for creative professionals.

How to Switch Themes

Changing themes is simple and takes just a few seconds.

  1. Open My Toolbox and go to the main dashboard
  2. Click the Settings icon (⚙️) in the top menu or sidebar
  3. Look for the Appearance section
  4. Find the Theme dropdown and select your preferred theme
  5. Click Apply or Save if prompted
  6. Restart My Toolbox to see the new theme in full effect
💡 Tip: You can also access theme settings from the Settings icon in your system tray.

Theme Tips & Recommendations

🌅 Daily Switching

Use Light theme during the day and Dark theme at night to reduce eye strain and adapt to your environment.

💼 Professional Look

For business environments or client presentations, stick with the Blue or Light theme for a polished, professional appearance.

🎨 Creative Work

The Purple theme is designed with creatives in mind — vibrant, energetic, and inspiring.

🌿 Productivity

The Green theme pairs well with productivity tools like the Timer and Stopwatch — it's calming yet motivating.

⚙️ Custom Combinations

Combine theme changes with font size and animation adjustments to create your perfect setup.

💡 Pro Tip: If you have a dark/OLED monitor, the Dark theme will save power and reduce eye strain significantly.

Accessibility & Contrast

All themes meet WCAG accessibility standards for color contrast and readability. If you have vision or color-blindness concerns:

  • High Contrast: Use Light or Dark theme (highest contrast)
  • Color Blindness: All themes avoid red/green reliance in critical UI elements
  • Font Size: Adjust font size in Settings for easier reading
  • Animations: Disable animations if they cause motion sickness
♿ Accessibility: My Toolbox is designed to be accessible to everyone. If you find any accessibility issues, please report them.

Advanced: Create Custom Themes (Future)

  • Import or export custom color palettes
